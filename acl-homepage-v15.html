What Facility Managers Get

Engineered for Consistent High-Quality Performance

Advantage Cleaning is not a standard janitorial company. Behind every shift sits a dedicated supervisor, a documented cleaning plan, and a digital quality system that flags any shortfall before your staff notices it. Whether you manage one building or a portfolio across the Tri-State area, the performance standard stays the same.

Most facility managers have seen the same pattern. A cleaning company starts strong, then loses discipline over the first year. By the time complaints reach you from staff or tenants, the decline has been going on for months. The root cause is almost always the same: no system holding performance to a standard. Individual cleaners fall back on habit. Supervisors too stretched to inspect every shift. Without data, there is no way to catch the slide before it becomes a problem.

Advantage Cleaning was built around that problem. Every shift runs against a documented cleaning plan. A dedicated supervisor inspects the work against your agreed KPIs and a performance report goes back to your team automatically. You get data, not assurances.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Advantage Cleaning

How does Advantage Cleaning ensure consistent results?

Every shift runs against a custom cleaning plan, with a supervisor scoring each zone and correcting deviations before the building opens. A weekly quality score report goes to your team automatically — no chasing required.

Can you maintain the same standard across multiple facilities?

Yes. Our process, people, and digital cleaning system follow identical standards whether you manage 1 facility or 20. Real-time reporting and quality metrics give you proof of performance across all facilities, consolidated in one weekly report.

What happens when something goes wrong?

Our supervisors catch deviations before you do. Issues are corrected in real time, logged in the system, and verified — so you never have to chase follow-up.

What size facilities do you serve?

We specialise in properties from 1,000 to over 1,000,000 sq ft — including schools, warehouses, and multi-property portfolios across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, New Jersey, and Connecticut.
