Questions About the Quality System

What Facility Managers Ask

What is the difference between a cleaning checklist and your quality system?

A checklist tells a cleaner what to do. Our quality system verifies that it was done — correctly, consistently, and on time. Every task is logged in real time. A dedicated supervisor inspects against your KPIs. Deviations are flagged and corrected before they become a complaint. The result is documented, not assumed.

What happens when something is not done correctly?

Our supervisor catches deviations before you do. Issues are corrected in real time, logged, and verified. Your weekly KPI report includes any exceptions flagged during the period, along with the corrective action taken. You are informed — not asked to act.

How does the quality system work across multiple buildings?

Every facility has its own custom cleaning plan specific to its compliance requirements, floor types, and traffic patterns. All plans run on the same quality system — same inspection framework, same KPI scoring, same escalation protocol. Your single account manager oversees all locations and delivers one consolidated weekly report.

How does Advantage Cleaning handle compliance documentation?

Compliance is embedded in your custom cleaning plan from day one. Every service generates documentation automatically — safety maintenance records, green cleaning product records, and supervisor sign-off. All records available on demand.

How quickly will we see results?

Most facility managers notice the difference within the first 30 days — fewer follow-up calls, fewer complaints, less mental overhead monitoring cleaning quality. By 90 days, the pattern is established: weekly reports arrive, issues are handled before you notice them, and compliance documentation is simply there when you need it.
