What Green Cleaning Means at Advantage Cleaning

We Use Green Cleaning Products as Standard. Not as an Add-On.

Most cleaning companies treat green cleaning as an upgrade tier — something clients have to request and pay extra for. Advantage Cleaning uses approved green cleaning products across every contract, in every facility type — schools, offices, warehouses, government buildings. It is how we work.

SDS sheets are maintained for every product in use. If your facility operates under specific green cleaning guidelines, we align to those requirements on a per-client basis during contract setup.

Product Standards

What “Green Cleaning Products” Means on Every Contract

Every product meets the criteria of government-approved green cleaning lists — lower VOC formulations, reduced environmental impact, safety data on file.

SDS Sheets — Every Product, Every Visit

Product SDS sheets maintained on file for every product in use. Product records generated with every service and available on request. If you need a specific format or records for a third party, your supervisor handles it directly.

Green Cleaning Records — Every Service

Green cleaning product records provided with every service. Supporting documentation for compliance records and parent information requests, available on demand.

Facility-Specific Alignment

If your facility operates under specific green cleaning guidelines — a particular product list, a documentation format, a reporting requirement — we align to those requirements during contract setup.

Integrated with the Quality System

Green cleaning products are tracked within the same quality system that manages every other aspect of service — supervisor verification, weekly KPI reports, and product records all flow through the same programme.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Facility Managers Ask About Green Cleaning

Is green cleaning included or an add-on?

Included — no add-on, no premium. Approved green cleaning products are standard on every Advantage Cleaning contract regardless of facility type. SDS sheets and product records are included with every service report.

What if our facility has specific green cleaning requirements?

We handle that during contract setup. Whether it’s a specific product list, a documentation format, or a reporting requirement, we align to it on a per-client basis. Bring it to the assessment and we’ll work through it with you.

What documentation is provided for green cleaning?

SDS sheets for every product in use, product records with every service. Available on request — if you need a specific format or records for a third party, contact your supervisor directly.

How is green cleaning tracked within the quality system?

Green cleaning products are tracked within the same quality system that manages every other aspect of service — supervisor verification, weekly KPI reports, and product records all flow through the same programme. It is not a separate compliance track. It is how we work.
