Class A Office Buildings

What Class A Actually Requires

Class A buildings use premium materials that most cleaning companies are not equipped to handle. Building prestige depends on every surface being maintained correctly.

Marble, Stone & Metal — Specialist Care for Premium Finishes

Class A office lobbies, elevator cabs, restrooms, and conference areas frequently feature marble floors, natural stone surfaces, and metal architectural elements. Using incorrect products on these materials causes permanent etching, tarnishing, or surface damage that cannot be reversed without costly restoration.

Our Marble & Stone specialists use stone-safe chemistry. Our Metal care technicians use surface-specific compounds and protective coatings. These roles are never interchangeable with general cleaning staff.

  • Marble, granite, and terrazzo — stone-safe daily care, periodic honing and sealing
  • Stainless steel, brass, bronze — polishing and protective coating application
  • Elevator cab interiors — full metal and surface restoration on schedule
  • Lobby feature walls and architectural elements — surface-specific protocols

Every building runs on the same quality system — same KPI framework, same supervisor verification, same consolidated weekly report. Your account manager oversees all locations. One call resolves any issue across the entire portfolio.

Compliance documentation is maintained and available on demand. Records cover safety, waste handling, and green cleaning across all buildings.

Yes — and these surfaces require specialist technicians, not general janitorial staff. Our Marble & Stone specialists use stone-safe chemistry. Our Metal care technicians use surface-specific compounds for stainless steel, brass, bronze, and aluminium. These roles are never interchangeable. Using the wrong product on marble causes permanent etching.

Yes. Green cleaning products are standard on every contract. Supporting documents are available on request.

The goal is that tenant complaints reach your account manager before they reach you. Our supervisor inspects every shift against agreed KPIs. If a tenant does raise an issue, your account manager handles it directly and confirms resolution with you. Most building clients tell us tenant cleaning complaints effectively stop within the first 30 days on contract.
