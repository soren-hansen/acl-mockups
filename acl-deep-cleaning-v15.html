Frequently Asked Questions

What Facility Managers Ask About Deep Cleaning

What is the difference between daily janitorial and deep cleaning?

Daily janitorial maintains visible surfaces on a routine schedule. Deep cleaning addresses the areas that routine cleaning does not reach — inside fixtures, under furniture, HVAC vents, grout lines, drain systems, and all high-touch surfaces with full dwell-time disinfection. It also covers surface-specific restoration work that is scheduled periodically. Deep cleaning is a capital maintenance function, not just a thorough version of daily service.

How often should a commercial building be deep cleaned?

A general programme for most commercial offices: electrostatic disinfection quarterly, restroom deep sanitising quarterly, floor restoration semi-annually, full-building deep clean annually. High-traffic buildings like school campuses, visitor attractions, and medical offices typically require more frequent scheduling. We assess the specific facility and recommend accordingly.

Can you respond to emergency disinfection situations?

Yes. Same-day emergency disinfection response is available for current contract clients and for facilities not yet on contract. Full electrostatic disinfection using EPA-registered products, with complete documentation produced for building management and tenant communication.

Can deep cleaning be scheduled outside business hours?

Yes, and for most services overnight or weekend scheduling is standard. Floor restoration chemicals require drying time before foot traffic. Electrostatic disinfection requires dwell time before re-entry. We schedule all services to align with your operational hours so the facility is fully ready for occupancy before business opens.
