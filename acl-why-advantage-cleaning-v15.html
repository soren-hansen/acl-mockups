What Competitors Don't Tell You

The Questions to Ask Every Cleaning Company Before You Sign

These questions reveal the gap between what a company claims and what it can actually deliver. Ask them of every vendor — including us. We will answer every one specifically.

Question 1
"What documentation do I receive after each service?"
Most answer: An invoice. Occasionally a completion confirmation. Ask for a sample service record.
Question 2
"How do you verify quality — and what is your escalation process when a standard isn't met?"
Most answer: Vague reference to supervisors. Ask for their inspection scoring methodology.
Question 3
"How long have you held your longest active contract — and can I speak to that client?"
Most answer: Hesitation. A reference list with no direct contacts. Ask for a named Facility Manager you can call directly — not a letter or a logo.
Question 4
"Can you provide OSHA compliance documentation for our facility?"
Most answer: "We follow all OSHA rules." Ask for the specific records they produce.
Question 5
"What EPA certifications do your products carry, and can I see the product list?"
Most answer: "We use green products." Ask for the EPA registration numbers for each product used in your facility.
Question 6
"Who is my single point of contact — and what is their direct number?"
Most answer: A general office number. Ask for the name of the specific person accountable for your account.
